Event Production – BUas AGM Staff Events

As part of the Production House project, I worked with a team to organize two major events for BUas AGM staff: a Thanksgiving gathering and a New Year’s drinks event. While I was primarily responsible for the marketing and volunteer coordination, I gained valuable insights into various other aspects of event planning.

My main responsibilities included developing and executing marketing strategies to promote the events, ensuring we reached the right audience effectively. I also managed volunteer coordination, ensuring the team was well-prepared and the events ran smoothly. Additionally, I collaborated closely with the team on event concept development, sourcing decorations within budget, and creating floor plans.

This project gave me hands-on experience in event marketing, team coordination, and logistics, while providing a broader understanding of what goes into delivering professional, high-quality events.
