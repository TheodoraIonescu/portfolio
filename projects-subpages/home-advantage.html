Home Advantage – Documentary Development & Marketing

As part of a documentary development project, our team created Home Advantage, a short documentary concept centered on the inspiring story of a disadvantaged girl playing in a field hockey club. The film aimed to highlight the resilience and joy of disabled athletes, challenging perceptions and showcasing that happiness and passion are not limited by physical difficulties.

My primary role in this project was developing a comprehensive marketing plan tailored to our documentary’s goals and budget. Using the SOSTAC method and relevant marketing theories, I devised strategic promotion ideas to maximize outreach and engagement. This process deepened my understanding of documentary marketing, refining my skills in audience targeting, promotional strategy, and budget allocation.

This project reinforced my passion for marketing and provided valuable insights into crafting an impactful promotional campaign for a socially driven film.
